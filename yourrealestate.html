The real estate you need
When you’re the CEO of a rising startup, real estate often takes a backseat to driving the business forward. But should it? Tech giants have started in a garage or a dorm room—unconventional spaces that have become part of the DIY mythos of Silicon Valley. But in reality, your space should be a platform for you to test, explore and scale, because your environment says a lot about you, and influences your thinking, your culture and your brand.

Whether you’re a firm of two, 25 or 100, here’s the rough guide to real estate that rolls with the punches.
